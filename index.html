The Chef

Stuti Desai

Stuti's professional journey began with a clear goal of studying Pharmacy but with an underlying passion for baking. She went on to complete her bachelors in India, from Institute of Chemical technology, while loading her family and friends, with all the sweets and treats she made. She went onto completing her masters in pharmaceutics from the University of Pittsburgh. Her lurking passion compelled her to learn and discover different facets of baking, throughout her journey. Ultimately, when she was faced with the dilemma of making a career, she chose to do what she would love to wake up every morning for- baking. Stuti pours her heart into baking and has an endless ambition to learn and explore new recipes as well as techniques. Via Ambroseutics, Stuti wishes to serve her passion on a plate.
